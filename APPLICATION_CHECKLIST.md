## 🚀 Application Submission Steps

1. **Go to**: https://cloud.google.com/startup/apply

2. **Select Tier**: "Start" (for $2,000 credits)

3. **Fill Out Form**:
   - Company information
   - Project description
   - Technology stack
   - Use of credits
   - Business model
   - Links to website/GitHub

4. **Upload Materials**:
   - Screenshots
   - Business plan (optional)
   - Pitch deck (optional)

5. **Review & Submit**:
   - Double-check all information
   - Verify all links work
   - Submit application

6. **Wait for Review**:
   - Typically 1-2 weeks
   - May receive follow-up questions
   - Approval notification via email

---

## 💡 Pro Tips for Approval

✅ **DO:**
- Show clear problem and solution
- Demonstrate technical competence (GitHub code)
- Have a realistic business model
- Show why Google Cloud is essential
- Be specific about credit usage
- Include working demo/website
- Respond quickly to follow-up questions

❌ **DON'T:**
- Submit incomplete applications
- Have broken links or dead websites
- Use generic/template descriptions
- Claim unrealistic growth projections
- Ignore eligibility requirements
- Submit without testing everything
- Use poor grammar or spelling
